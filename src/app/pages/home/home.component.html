WHAT ARE THE FINILIAR

The Finilar are a series of cute avatars that respond to the flow of data, becoming happy and healthy as value increases and depressed, sad and possibly dying as it falls. In this way the Finilar might be said to be alive, the manifestation of digital entities, many of which touch our day to day lives as the value of a currency or company fluctuates.

A LIVING NFT

The Finiliar are first an art work, existing as living NFTs feeding off each crypto currency, second by second, minute by minute. You’ll be able to tell in a glance how your favourite coin is doing, whether you should buy more to help out your new friend or possibly divest and abandon the Finiliar to their fate.

ALWAYS AVAILABLE FOR YOU

There will only ever be one Finiliar for any currency, more than just an mascot the Finiliar are in a way the living embodiment of the data the represent. Finiliar by their nature love the market, each will always be exposed to the market, always for sale, with the successful bidder able to set the next price. In this way you’ll always have an opportunity to own one of these critters.

PERKS OF A FINILIAR

There are various perks to being an owner of a Finilar, these include but not limited to access to sculptural avatars, access to the Finilars in Decentraland as well as much much more. The hope is that with your help the Finiliarverse can grow and grow and grow.

ALWAYS AVAILABLE FOR YOU

Finiliar also love the market, each Finiliar is always exposed to the market, always for sale, with the successful bidder able to set the next price, meaning you’ll always have a chance to own one of these critters.
