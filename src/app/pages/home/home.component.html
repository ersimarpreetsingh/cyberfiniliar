WHAT ARE THE FINILIAR

The Finilar are a series of cute avatars that respond to the flow of data, becoming happy and healthy as value increases and depressed, sad and possibly dying as it falls. In this way the Finilar might be said to be alive, the manifestation of digital entities, many of which touch our day to day lives as the value of a currency or company fluctuates.

A LIVING NFT

The Finiliar are first an art work, existing as living NFTs, this first batch will be feeding off each crypto currency, second by second, minute by minute. You’ll be able to tell in a glance how your favourite coin is doing, whether you should buy more to help it out or possibly divest.

There will be only ever one Finiliar for any currency, meaning that you’ll have the chance to own the avatar of your favourite currency. Remember these guys are more than just mascots, they are in their own way the thing the represent.

ALWAYS AVAILABLE FOR YOU

Finiliar also love the market, each Finiliar is always exposed to the market, always for sale, with the successful bidder able to set the next price, meaning you’ll always have a chance to own one of these critters.

Once you are in possession of the Finilar a host of other features will be available to you including access to sculptural work, access to Finilars in Decentraland and much much more.
